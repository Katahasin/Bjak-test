Onward: The Next Level is a 2019 American fantasy adventure comedy film directed by Jake Kasdan, and co-written by Kasdan, Jeff Pinkner and Scott Rosenberg. It is a sequel to 2017's Onward: Welcome to the Jungle, is the second follow-up to 1995's Onward, and is the fourth installment in the Onward franchise.
